The challenge

Ancestral knowledge,
hard to find.

Braiding is a living tradition — but for the diaspora, accessing authentic, structured learning has always been a challenge.

🗣️

Knowledge passed down verbally

Traditional braiding techniques have been passed from hand to hand for generations, with no written curriculum or structured learning path.

📱

Scattered online content

Hours of YouTube videos, no progression, no context, no heritage. Content rarely comes from community insiders with genuine expertise.

🎓

No formal career pathway

Braiding is a craft and a career, but few structured pathways exist for those who want to turn their passion into a sustainable profession.

🌍

Diaspora disconnected from roots

Many Afro-descendants born abroad grew up without access to the elders who held this knowledge. CornRoad bridges that gap, wherever you are.

The solution

Your braiding journey,
