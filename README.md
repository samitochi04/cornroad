# CornRoad
CornRoad is the afro-educational platform where African and Afro-descendant people learn braiding and styling — from first cornrow to professional mastery.

## Features
- Step-by-step video tutorials by expert braiders
- Progressive curriculum from complete beginner to pro
- Rooted in African hair traditions and cultural history
- Community of learners, instructors and mentors
- Track your progress and earn sharable certificates
